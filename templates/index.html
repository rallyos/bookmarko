Get excited about bookmarking

Easy to use interface

The interface is simple, it uses some design principles from mail clients. You can create groups and move bookmarks to them with drag and drop. Managing your saved sites happens with just a couple of clicks.

Quick search and tags

Finding the site that you saved months before is a joy. The search bar offers fast and smart way to look through your collection. You can also tag bookmarks with keywords for better search experience.

Multiple ways to view your saved sites

Manage your content in the way that suits you best. You can choose between three views, and every one has it's own advantage. The third view shows some information about the saved sites, and presents them in a nice grid.

Optimized for most devices

Bookmarko was made with responsive design in mind. It was build to support wide range of devices. The site is optimized for touch input, so you can access and use it from your mobile device.

Beta

Bookmarko is still in beta and will be a always developing project. There are still many things to come.

The site is using Google App Engine for back-end, which offers us scalability and other cool things.
